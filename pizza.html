Pizza recipe

1) Make the dough

  • Mix 1.5 cups flour, 1 tsp yeast, 1 tsp sugar, and 0.5 tsp salt.
  • Stir in 0.5 cup warm water and 1 tbsp olive oil.
  • Knead for 5 minutes until smooth.
  • Cover and let rise for 1 hour.

2) Make the sauce

  • Mix 1 cup crushed tomatoes with 1 minced garlic clove
  • Stir in 1 tsp olive oil and 0.5 tsp dried oregano.
  • Add a pinch of salt.
  • Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes.

3) Assemble the pizza

  • Preheat your oven to 250°C (500°F).
  • Stretch the dough into a round circle on a baking sheet.
  • Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough.
  • Scatter shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings on top.

4) Bake the pizza

  1. Place the baking sheet into the hot oven.
  2. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Remove when the crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
  4. Cool for 2 minutes, slice, and serve.
