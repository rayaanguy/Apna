My Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • Greens: 4 cups romaine lettuce or spinach (washed and dried)
  • Vegetables: 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce thoroughly
  2. Chop the lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber
  3. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Toss the vegetables in the dressing and serve immediately
