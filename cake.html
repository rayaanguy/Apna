Chocolate Cake recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1.5 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1.5 cup vegetable oil or any other neutral oil
  • 1.5 cup milk (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 0.5 cup boiling water

Instructions;

  1. Prep: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease an 8-inch or 9-inch round cake pan with butter and lightly dust it with flour.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until there are no lumps.

  3. Add Wet Ingredients: Make a well in the center of the dry mix and add the eggs, oil, milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth.

  4. Add Hot Water: Carefully pour in the boiling hot water and mix gently. (The batter will be thin, which keeps the cake incredibly moist)

  5. Bake: Pour the batter into your prepared pan. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.

  6. Cool & Frost: Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before adding frosting.
